# Asset & Icon Specifications

Use Lucide or equivalent outline SVG icons, 2px stroke, inherit `currentColor`.

Icons: Calendar, Folder, Search, ChevronLeft, ChevronRight, Plus, X, MoreVertical, Upload, FileText, Users, Clock, MapPin, Trash. Sizes: nav 20px, controls 18px, inline/file 16px.

Logo: optional text mark `WorkSpace`, black on white. No photography, no illustrations except simple outline empty-state icon.
