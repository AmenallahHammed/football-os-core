What is Football OS?

Football OS is a connected platform for football clubs. It brings the main operational, sporting, and analytical workflows of the club into one system.

Instead of using disconnected tools, clubs use Football OS as a shared operating layer across collaboration, training, analysis, player experience, recruitment, and administration.

Why clubs change

Why clubs need a connected platform

Most clubs still work across disconnected tools, separate departments, and fragmented workflows. Football OS gives the club one shared operating layer instead of a growing patchwork of systems.

Fragmented information

Documents, files, and working notes often live in different places.

Disconnected coordination

Calendars, staff updates, and daily planning are rarely aligned in one workflow.

Separate sporting workflows

Coaches, analysts, and performance staff often work in parallel instead of together.

Isolated performance context

Performance data and video analysis are not always connected to day-to-day operations.

Weak player access

Players often lack one clear digital touchpoint for information, schedules, and updates.

Recruitment without continuity

Scouting and recruitment decisions are harder to connect back to shared club data.
